Important Tips

  • files are merged in the order you see in front of you starting from the top left corner going right.
  • you can drag and drop files to change the order.
  • page range is not required
  • page range accepts two numbers separated by a dash "-" or a single number for one page.
  • Your files are uploaded to the server
  • Your files will be deleted one hour after your first upload
  • Another page for complex file operations will be created in the future
